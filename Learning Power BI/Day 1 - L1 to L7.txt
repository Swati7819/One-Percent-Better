L6: What is Business Intelligence?

1. We have lot of data these days.

2. Business intelligence is all about harnessing the data that our business generates so we can analyze and visualize it to gain insights on how our business is performing.

3.  According to Forrester research, BI is a set of methodologies, processes, architecture and technologies that transform raw data into meaningful and useful information used to enable more effective strategic, tactical and operational insights and decision making.

4. Basically, BI is to deliver relevant and reliable information to the management at the right time so our management makes better business decisions.  BI is also known as decision support system. 

L7: Why use BI?

1. Accelerating and improving decision making.
2. Optimize internal process.
3. Getting to know business past, present and future.
4. Increasing operational efficiency.
5. Giving quicker responses to business related queries.
6. No more guess work.
7. Meeting/exceeding manufacturing and inventory goals.
8. Gaining valuable insights into customer's behavior.
9. And even more.  :)
