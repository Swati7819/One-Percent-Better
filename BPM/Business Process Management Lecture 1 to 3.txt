Lecture 3: What is Business Process Management?

Acc to Wikipedia, It is a management science that focuses on improving corporate performance by managing and optimizing a company's business processes.
 
BPM was a buzz word within industry which has led to a lot of confusion. However in 2014, It become a clearly defined methodology.

what it is?

A method for managing processes.
A means to identify which processes are executed within an org.
A business led methodology or project.
The foundation of facilitating a continuous improvement culture within a team or organization.

What it is not?

A pure process improvement methodology (but it provides the means for process management).
 
An IT led solution.
  
A tactical tool(BPM is a strategic led,  operationally deployed).

There are the most common mistakes companies and people make when it cokes to BPM:

Mistake 1: Jumping straight into modelling the process before doing anything else.

Mistake 2: Not understanding the functionality of BPMN(Business Process Management and Notation) and where it fits in within the broader BPM discipline.

Mistake 3: Not able to explain BPM to stakeholders or people without a BPM background, or not educating them in the first place.

Mistake 4: Not getting executive endorsement before embarking on a BPM project. 

Mistake 5: No use of layering or application of a process architecture.

Mistake 6: Confusing BPM with business process improvement methodologies such as Lean or Six Sigma.
